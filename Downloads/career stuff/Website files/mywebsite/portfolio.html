Bit Bit Revenge: Purdue University-Microprocessors and Interfacing

Bit Bit revenge is a hardware spin off of the classic iPhone game “Tap Tap Revenge”. It comprises of four columns of steaming bubbles that the player has to click as they flow across the screen. The whole premise of the game is to get as many points as possible within the allotted time period. Generally, the frequency of the bubbles scrolling down the columns is directly proportionally to the background music chosen by the player. In our version of this iconic game, we used a handmade 8 x 4 LED array that is used to scroll through the lights. As the players clicks through the buttons he/she accumulates a score that is displayed on the LCD screen. Click to see the demo
